히키-신 키타 멜로듸 Vinyl Pressings Guide

A list of all known unique pressings of Shin Joong Hyun's first album, Hikishin Guitar Melody (히키-신 키타 멜로듸).

This was the only known record Shin Joong Hyun released before his time with The Add4, and he still went under the name of Hikishin (Jacky Shin) even during his Add4 days.
