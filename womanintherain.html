비속의 여인 Vinyl Pressings Guide

A list of all known unique pressings of The Add4's first album, Woman In The Rain (비속의 여인).

Release Front Sleeve Back Sleeve A-Side Label B-Side Label Record Label Catalogue No. Format Additional Info
1964/12 LKL LKL-1014 LP 1st pressing, featuring a sleeve with the band on the KBS-TV stage.
1964? LKL LKL-1014 LP 2nd pressing, featuring a photo of the lead singer Seo Jeong Gil.
1964? LKL LKL-1014 LP Variation on the 2nd pressings. Front sleeve features a white LKL Records logo and boxes for song titles.
1964? LKL LKL-1014 LP Label variation on the 2nd pressings, featuring a grey label.
1964? LKL LKL-1014 LP Variation on the 2nd pressings, featurings two orange strips on the back sleeve.
1970? Shinjin 1014 LP 3rd pressing, features red coloured boxes. Some of the 3rd, 4th, and 5th pressings were on coloured vinyl.
1970? Shinjin 1014 LP 4th pressing, features orange coloured boxes.
1970? Shinjin 114 LP 5th pressing, featuring text above the front sleeve that reads "Shin Joong Hyun First Composition Album".
1970/12/25 Shinjin 1014 LP Label variation on the 5th pressings.
1970? Shinjin LK 1014 LP 6th pressing, side A features a shuffled tracklist, whilst side B is taken from Lee Dong Ki's album featuring Seo Jeong Gil from the Add4.
2006/06/14 RythmOn GEBL-SL06 LP 7th pressing, from a box set with the Hikishin album. Yellow vinyl. Limited to 300 copies.
2007/10/24 Pony Canyon SJHMVD 2619 CD 1st CD pressing. Digipak.
2011/10/21 Pony Canyon JHSGS001 CD 2nd CD pressing. Digipak jewel case hybrid packaging.
2017/10/25 RythmOn GEBL-SL62 LP 8th pressing. Transparent yellow vinyl. Limited to 500 copies.
